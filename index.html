I Love Penguins

Penguins are beautiful creatures. They are great swimmers, have the cutest walk, and are loyal and hopeless romantics, so much so that they are a source of inspiration for movies, series, and documentaries. When watching a show featuring penguins, it becomes impossible not to fall in love with them.
